History

AJ Frank FX was founded in 2022 by AJ Frank, a seasoned forex trader with over a decade of experience in the financial markets. AJ's passion for trading and education led him to create a platform where he could share his knowledge and skills with others. Starting as a small community of traders, AJ Frank FX has grown into a reputable online academy, offering comprehensive forex education and resources to traders worldwide.

Services:

AJ Frank FX offers a range of services to cater to different levels of traders:
  • Forex Education:* In-depth online courses, video lessons, and interactive resources covering beginner to advanced topics.

  • Trading Strategies:* Proven and tested strategies for various market conditions, including scalping, day trading, and swing trading.

  • Market Analysis:* Regular market updates, analysis, and insights to help traders stay informed and make informed decisions.

  • Mentorship:* One-on-one coaching and personalized guidance for traders seeking tailored support.

  • Community:* Active community forum and social media groups for traders to connect, share ideas, and learn from each other.

  • Trading Tools:* Access to proprietary trading tools, indicators, and software to enhance trading performance.

  • Account Management:* Managed accounts and trade copying services for those who prefer a hands-off approach.

  • Webinars and Events:* Regular webinars, workshops, and live trading sessions with AJ Frank and guest experts.

Mission:

AJ Frank FX aims to empower traders with the knowledge, skills, and resources needed to succeed in the forex market. By providing high-quality education, support, and community, AJ Frank FX helps traders achieve their financial goals and become confident, independent traders.
